Meet our Trainers!

Karl Duignan

I am the Fitness Coordinator for Personal Training & Assessments and owner at KD Fitness. I received a BS in Health, Physical Education & Exercise Science from Virginia Commonwealth University and am currently working on my MS in Health Promotion & Exercise Science from California University of Pennsylvania. I am certified in strength and conditioning specialist through the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA), a certified health fitness specialist through the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM), and a certified health coach through the American Council on Exercise (ACE). I have experience working with a wide variety of populations through many different fitness domains. I enjoy all aspects of fitness and am eager to spread that passion to others!

Leandro Silva

Leo is a graduate of AU and studied justice and law. He regards fitness as the most important aspect of his life. leo has been teaching fitness classes such as Sculpt & Tone, Killer Kettlebells and Indoor Cycling since August 2014. He has been a certified personal trainer with the American Council on Exercise (ACE) since December 2014. He enjoys working out, competing in power lifting competitions and spending time helping others reach their fitness goals.

Mahamed Basha

Basha is a certified personal trainer through the American Council on Exercise (ACE). He is currently working towards his Health Promotion degree here at American University. He has Strength and Conditioning experience working with AU athletes. Basha has been active his whole life, playing on both club and school sports teams in High School. He enjoys exercise and believes that physical activity is a large portion of overall wellness.
